mySKIN - modernizing your skincare routine.

Currently, I am in the beginning stages of developing an app that will allow users to submit a form identifying their skin type and potential skin issues. Users will then be provided dermotologist recommended skincare products, based on what ingredients are known to best treat the customers indicated skincare issues. The goal of this project is to build on my prior app development experience. Previously, I developed the LocationSearch app, an application that used google's map API to give users nearby shops and activities.

Binary Blackhole Simulation

As a research student, I worked with a PhD level team to simulate data for merging binary blackholes and the energy released due to gravitational waves. I used a linux OS, python, Anaconda, and VisIt visualization software to produce animations of the analyzed data. Space exploration and technology is still something I'm passionate about exploring!

Mars Rover and Resource Transportation Simulation

While on my second research team at Georgia Tech, I contributed to the creation of a Mars rover prototype designed to excavate and transport martian soil and other potential resources. Additionally, I aided in the design of a MatLab simulation based on resource composition to determine the miminum amount of materials that would need to be intitally transported to Mars (in a hypothetical mars mission) in order to develop additional resources later on.
